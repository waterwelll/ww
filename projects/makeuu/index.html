BEAUTY PROFILE

Elena Vasquez:
Crafting Beauty for Fashion's Elite

By Sophie Laurent November 2023

With over 12 years in the beauty industry, Elena Vasquez has become the secret weapon behind countless iconic beauty moments. Her portfolio includes exclusive collaborations with Hermosa, Elena, and Zara for their runway shows, where her innovative techniques translate designers' visions into living art.

On commercial sets, Vasquez's technical mastery shines through looks engineered to withstand hours under hot lights while maintaining pixel-perfect precision. Her campaign work for L'Oréal, Maybelline, and Revlon has redefined commercial beauty standards.

"Every face is a canvas and every project an opportunity to create something extraordinary. The runway demands artistry, while commercials require technical perfection."

THE ART OF EDITORIAL MAKEUP

Editorial makeup transcends traditional beauty standards, creating visual stories that captivate and inspire. In my work for Vogue Italia, we explored the interplay of light, texture, and bold color choices—each look a carefully constructed narrative element. This series represents the pinnacle of collaborative creativity between makeup artists, photographers, and stylists.
